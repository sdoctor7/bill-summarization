the payer state transparency act of 2015 .
this bill directs the department of commerce to calculate the federal tax burden of each state for each calendar year. requires the secretary to treat federal taxes paid by an individual as a burden on the state in which such individual occurs, and treat federal taxes paid by a legal business entity as a burden on each state in which economic activity of such entity is performed in the same proportion that the economic activity of such entity in all the state.
