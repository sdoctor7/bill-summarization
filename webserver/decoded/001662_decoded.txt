higher education reform and opportunity act of 2015 .
this bill amends title iv (student assistance) of the higher education act of 1965 to require the department of education (ed) to establish an alternative accreditation system for the purpose of establishing institutions that provide postsecondary education and postsecondary education courses or programs as eligible for funding under title iv if the state enters into an agreement with the va for the establishment of the alternative accreditation program.
