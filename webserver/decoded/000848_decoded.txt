transparency and accountability act of 2017 .
this bill amends the public health service act to revise the united states preventive services task force to establish the united states preventive services task force to establish and periodically convene the united states preventive services task plan. the task force must review the scientific evidence and new science related to the effectiveness and appropriateness of clinical preventive services for the purpose of developing recommendations for primary care clinicians and the health care community and updating previous clinical preventive property.
