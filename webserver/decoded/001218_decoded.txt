safeguarding classrooms hurt by [UNK] obligatory levies - amends the internal revenue code, with respect to the employer health insurance credit, to: (1) exempt local educational agency or state educational agency any institution of higher education, and (2) study the impact of the employer health insurance mandate for effect on the day before the date of enactment of this act and the ability of state educational programs, local educational agencies and secondary schools to meet the purposes of the elementary and secondary education act of 1965.
