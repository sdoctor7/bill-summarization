temporary judgeship conversion act of 2016 .
this bill authorizes the existing judgeships for the district of kansas and the eastern district of missouri authorized by the judicial improvements of 1990 and the existing judgeships for the eastern district of mexico, the district of columbia, the central district of california, the western district of wyoming, the western district of engineers, the western district of wyoming, the authorized as of the effective date enactment of this act, the district of columbia, the northern district of columbia, the western district of north carolina, and the district of new mexico to be authorized
