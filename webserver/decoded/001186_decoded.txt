sequestration tied to member pay [UNK] act of 2013 - amends the legislative reorganization act of 1946 to eliminate the annual rate of compensation of members of congress by a percentage equal to the effective reduction in the average annual rate of pay of employees of the federal government is reduced as a result of a candidate, related to the average percentage reduction in such effective annual rate of pay during those 2 fiscal years for all employees of the federal government whose pay was reduced as a result of such a loan.
