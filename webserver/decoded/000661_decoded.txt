this bill amends the national defense authorization act for fiscal year 2013 to direct the secretary of defense (dod) to ensure that each affected eligible beneficiary who is enrolled in tricare prime as of september 30, 2013, to make a one-time election to continue such enrollment in tricare prime notwithstanding that such contract does not allow for such enrollment based on the location in which such beneficiary period. the beneficiary shall continue such enrollment in tricare prime so long as the beneficiary resides in the same zip code in which the beneficiary resided at the time of such service.
