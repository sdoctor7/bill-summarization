this bill directs the department of veterans affairs (va) to sell or continue to furnish the patient psychiatric care at the facility of the department of veterans affairs (va), and has the capacity and capability to provide such services. includes for purposes of determining the rates of reimbursement for a non-department facility that furnishes such facility as a health care provider being reimbursed pursuant to the veterans access, choice, and accountability act of 1965.
