the american indian trust responsibility review act of 2015 .
this bill establishes the american indian trust review commission [UNK] the american indian trust review commission. the bill establishes the american indian trust review commission to provide a review of the current state of the united states unique trust relationship with federally recognized indian tribes and their members in order to better administer constitutional trust responsibilities and make necessary revisions in relevant trust programs, regulations and policies for the benefit of american indian businesses.
