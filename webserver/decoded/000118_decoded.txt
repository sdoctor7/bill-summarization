electric transmission infrastructure permitting improvement act .
this bill establishes the interagency rapid response team for fy2015 to expedite and improve the permitting process for electric transmission infrastructure on federal land and non-federal lands. requires the mission of the team to include: (1) to improve the timeliness and efficiency of electric transmission infrastructure [UNK] and (2) facilitate the performance of maintenance and upgrades to electric transmission lines on federal land and non-federal lands. the team shall: .
