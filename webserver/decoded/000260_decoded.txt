aco improvement act of 2016 .
this bill amends title xviii (medicare) of the social security act to require the department of health and human services to permit an aco to: (1) reduce or eliminate cost-sharing otherwise applicable under part b for some or all primary care services furnished by the health care professionals [UNK] professionals furnishing services through a rural health clinic or federally qualified health care, and (2) develop additional incentives to encourage patient engagement and participation in their own consumers.
