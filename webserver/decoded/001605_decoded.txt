the regulatory predictability for business growth act of 2017 .
this bill amends the federal criminal code to establish a longstanding interpretative rule that has an interpretative rule that has been in effect for at least one year and that is: (1) altering or is in any way inconsistent with, any provision in a subsequently promulgated interpretative requirements, and (2) an interpretative rule of an agency, unless the interpretative rule revises a longstanding interpretative rule of the agency to an interpretative rule that has been in effect for at least one year.
