sbic advisers relief act of 2015 .
this bill amends the investment advisers act of 1940 to provide that a venture capital fund is an entity to be regulated as a business development company under the investment advisers act of 1940 to eliminate: (1) a venture capital fund includes an entity to be regulated or is regulated as a business development company agreement, and (2) the assets under management of a private fund that is an entity to be regulated or is regulated as a business development company program.
