h-1b and l-1 visa reform act of 2013 - amends the immigration and nationality act to revise the h-1b visa fraud and abuse protections for purposes of aliens working temporarily in the united states. amends the immigration and nationality act to revise requirements for h-1b visa fraud and abuse protections for aliens working temporarily in the united states. amends the immigration and nationality act to revise requirements for h-1b visa fraud and abuse protections for h-1b employer application requirements.
