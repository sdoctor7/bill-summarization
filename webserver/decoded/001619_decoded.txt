amends the internal revenue code, with respect to the tax credit for tax credit, to establish a program to which taxpayers may download forms relating to the individual income tax returns that are populated with return information reported to the department of health and human services (hhs). directs the secretary of the interior to make such return information available in both a printable document file for manual completion and filing and in a computer-readable form suitable for use by automated tax preparation services.
