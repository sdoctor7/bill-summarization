ltch technical correction act of 2015 .
this bill amends the federal food, drug, and schip extension act of 2007 to require the department of health and human services (hhs) to reduce the medicare long-term care hospital moratorium exception to the medicare long-term care hospital moratorium program. amends the medicare long-term care hospital moratorium to require the secretary of medicare to reduce the standard federal payment rate as if the estimated aggregate amount of high cost outlier payments for standard federal payment rate discharges for each such fiscal year.
