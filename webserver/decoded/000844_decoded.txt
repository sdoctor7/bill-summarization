the social security caregiver credit act of 2016 .
this bill amends title ii (old age, survivors, and disability insurance) (oasdi) of the social security act to direct the secretary of health and human services to address the unfair exclusion of professional and hardworking home care providers who are not eligible to receive social security or medicare because they provide paid care to a family member with a disability under programs operated at the state and local level for general health and welfare protection purposes.
