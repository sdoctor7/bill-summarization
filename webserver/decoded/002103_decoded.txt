the e-verify modernization act of 2013 - amends the illegal immigration reform and immigrant responsibility act of 1996 to require every person or other entity that hires one or more individuals for employment in the united states to elect to participate in the e-verify program every person or other entity that hires one or more individuals for employment in the united states shall elect to participate in the e-verify program and shall comply with the terms and conditions of such an individual's election to work in the united states. amends the illegal immigration reform and immigrant responsibility act of 1996
