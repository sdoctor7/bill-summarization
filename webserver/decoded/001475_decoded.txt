long term care insurance integrity act of 2013 - prohibits an insurance issuer that offers a long term care insurance plan from being designed to expeditiously resolve disputes concerning claims under the plan process. requires a claims dispute resolution procedure to: (1) be designed to expeditiously resolve disputes concerning claims under the plan program; (2) be designed to expeditiously resolve disputes concerning claims under the plan program; and (3) ensure that an enrollee is eligible to obtain claims review only to the extent and in the manner provided for in the applicable insurance period.
