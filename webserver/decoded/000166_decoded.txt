the airport and airway extension act of 2015 .
title [UNK] and airway programs airport improvement program - (sec. 101) amends the internal revenue code, with respect to the airport improvement tax credit, to: (1) extend such authority through september 30, 2016, to remain available until expended for the federal aviation administration and the u.s. act; and (2) for the period beginning on october 1, 2015, and shall remain available until expended for the period beginning on october 1, 2015, and ending on march 31, 2016.
