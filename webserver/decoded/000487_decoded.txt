the fair pay for presidential protection act .
this bill provides an increase in premium pay for certain federal employees performing protective services during any year in which a presidential election is held to an employee who performs work authorized by this act. prohibits any premium pay earned in a pay period subject to such a pay shall not be considered or creditable as basic pay for purposes of retirement or any other purpose and shall not be used in computing payment for accumulated and accrued annual leave in effect at the end of the calendar year.
