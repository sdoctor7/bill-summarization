the national security rocket engine study act of 2013 - directs the secretary of defense (dod) to submit to the congressional defense committees a report on the russian rd-180 rocket [UNK] requires the report to include the following: (1) an assessment of the feasibility of manufacturing an alternative rocket engine and (2) an estimate of the cost of manufacturing an alternative rocket engine in the united states, including any cost savings that manufacturing a domestic alternative provides compared to the cost of buying russian engines over the life of the evolved expendable launch vehicle law.
