southern energy access jobs act or the sea jobs act .
this bill directs the department of the interior to promulgate regulations that establish management of the surface occupancy of each portion of the south atlantic planning area for the applicable coastline of a state for any lease sale authorized under the outer continental shelf lands act. directs the secretary of the interior to promulgate regulations that establish management of the surface occupancy of each portion of the south atlantic planning area for the applicable coastline of a state for any lease sale authorized under the workforce investment act.
