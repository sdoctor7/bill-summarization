the responsible gse affordable housing investment act of 2014 - amends the federal housing enterprises financial safety and soundness act of 1992 to require the director of the federal housing enterprises financial safety and soundness act to establish standards for the multifamily special affordable housing goal that proportionately reduce the number of units receiving credit for any mortgage purchase by an enterprise that facilitates the conversion of dwelling units are available for rental at rates that are affordable to low-income families to dwelling units that would be affordable to low-income consumers.
