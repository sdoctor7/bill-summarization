tribe klamath river chinook salmon emergency disaster assistance act of 2017 .
this bill makes funds available for fisheries resource disaster assistance for the economic and social effects of the 2016 yurok tribe klamath river chinook salmon fishery disaster as determined by the secretary of commerce on january 20, 2015, and to mitigate the economic losses to such laws, articles, materials, or other entities caused by fishing closures or other restrictions on it.
