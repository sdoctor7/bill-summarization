conditions protection act of 2017 .
this bill amends the public health service act to require a group health plan or a health insurance issuer offering group health insurance coverage from being treated as a pre-existing condition in the absence of a diagnosis related to such services. a group health plan or a health insurance issuer offering group health insurance coverage may not impose any [UNK] existing condition exclusion with respect to a condition based on the fact that the condition was present before the date of enrollment in such plan or for such services.
