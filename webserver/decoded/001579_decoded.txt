rural telecommunications and broadband service act of 2017 .
this bill amends the communications act of 1934 to direct the rural telecommunications and broadband advisory committee to provide advice to the commission and reports to congress with respect to policy issues relating to telecommunications services in rural areas, including advice and recommendations that would promote the deployment of advanced and next telecommunications services in rural areas, and advise the commission with respect to the impact that agenda items of the commission could have on the provision of telecommunications services in rural areas.
