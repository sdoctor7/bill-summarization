the judicial transparency and ethics enhancement act of 2013 - amends the federal criminal code to require the head of the office of inspector general to be appointed by the chief justice of the united states after consultation with the majority and minority leaders of the senate and the speaker of the inspector general to serve for a term of 4 years and may be reappointed of alleged misconduct in the united states supreme court that may require oversight or other action within the judicial branch or by canada.
