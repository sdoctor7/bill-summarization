homeless children and youth act of 2014 - amends title iv (student assistance) of the mckinney-vento homeless assistance act to require the secretary of health and human services (hhs) to establish a homeless children program to unaccompanied youth and homeless families with children and youth defined as homeless under other federal statutes who are certified as homeless by the director or designee of a director of a program funded under any other federal facility, or have been certified by a director of a program funded under this act or a director or designee of a director of a program funded
