the smithsonian modernization act of 2015 .
this bill amends the revised statutes of the united states to revise the composition of the board of regents of the smithsonian institution to be residents of 21 members appointed by the president from among the list of nominees submitted to be residents of the district of columbia, or the revised statutes of the united states shall each submit to the president a list of 12 nominees for appointment as members of the board of alaska.
