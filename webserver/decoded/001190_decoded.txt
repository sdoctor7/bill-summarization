countering terrorist radicalization act .
this bill directs the department of homeland security (dhs) to provide training for planning, tribal, and territorial representatives at state and major urban area fusion centers for the purpose of administering community awareness briefings and related activities in furtherance of the efforts to counter violent extremism, identify and report suspicious activities, and increase awareness of and more quickly identify terrorism threats, including the travel or attempted travel of individuals from the united states to support a foreign terrorist organization.
