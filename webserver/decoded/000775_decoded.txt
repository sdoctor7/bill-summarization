veterans' health care integrity act of 2017 .
this bill directs the department of veterans affairs (va) to ensure that the director of each medical facility annually certifies to the secretary that the medical facility is in full compliance with all regulations and other provisions of law relating to scheduling appointments for veterans to receive hospital care or medical services, including veterans health administration directive 1230 or any successor facility, and any official data on wait times for appointments to receive hospital care or medical services submitted by the director to the secretary during the year preceding the submittal of
