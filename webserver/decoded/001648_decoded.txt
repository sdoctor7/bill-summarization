medicare audiology services enhancement act of 2013 - amends title xviii (medicare) of the social security act to provide comprehensive audiology services to medicare beneficiaries or [UNK] (1) hearing and balance assessment services; auditory treatment services, including auditory processing and auditory rehabilitation facilities; and (2) payment under part fee schedule for comprehensive audiology services. amends the social security act to provide audiology services performed in hospitals excluded from inpatient hospital services.
