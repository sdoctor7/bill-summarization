sbic advisers relief act of 2014 - amends the investment advisers act of 1940 to require a venture capital fund to include a venture capital fund includes an entity to be regulated or is regulated as a business development company pursuant to the investment advisers act of 1973. the investment advisers act of 1940 is amended to eliminate: (1) the assets under management of a private fund that is an entity to be regulated or is regulated as a business development company program, and (2) the assets under management of a private fund that is an entity to be regulated
