equal access to banking act .
this bill directs the federal deposit insurance corporation to issue regulations to: (1) easing the restrictive impact of regulations issued pursuant to the factors to be considered for deposit care, on non-profit community development financial institution programs; and easing restrictions on the operation of [UNK] community development financial institution banks insured by the federal deposit insurance corporation, and (2) ensure that regulations issued will not provide an opportunity for individuals or for-profit financial institutions to avoid taxation or allow charitable contributions and grants to non-profit community development financial institution services.
