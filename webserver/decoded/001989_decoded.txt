the government neutrality in contracting act - requires the head of each executive agency that awards any construction contract after the date of enactment of this act, or that obligates funds pursuant to such a contract to ensure that the agency and any construction manager acting on behalf of the federal government with respect to such individual's specifications in its bid control, project agreements, or other controlling documents does not: (1) require or prohibit a firearm, dealer, or subcontractor from entering in, or adhering to, agreements with 1 or more labor organizations, with respect to that construction project or another
