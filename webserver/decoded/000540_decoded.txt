the defund obamacare act of 2013 - prohibits the federal funds from being made available to carry out the provisions of the patient protection and affordable care act or the health care and education reconciliation act of 2010, or title i of the health care and education reconciliation act of 2010, or title i of the health care and education reconciliation act of 2010, or restores provisions of the patient protection and affordable care act or the health care and education reconciliation act of 2010, no entitlement to benefits under the health care and education reconciliation act of 2010.
