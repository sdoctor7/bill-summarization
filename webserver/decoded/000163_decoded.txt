autism assistance act of 2013 - directs the secretary of health and human services (hhs) to establish a health and education grant program related to autism spectrum disorders to enable them to autism spectrum patients. requires the secretary of health and human services (hhs) to carry out a pilot program to provide health and education grant program to autism spectrum disorders in the united states. requires the secretary of health and human services (hhs) to award competitive grants to local educational agencies (leas) to establish a global autism assistance program.
