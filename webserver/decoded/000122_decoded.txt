investment in vital energy reauthorization act or the river act .
this bill amends the energy policy act of 2005 to reauthorize hydroelectric production incentives and hydroelectric efficiency improvement program. the bill also amends title iv (student assistance) of the energy policy act of 2005 to expand hydroelectric production incentives and hydroelectric efficiency improvement program. the bill also amends the energy policy act of 2005 to reauthorize hydroelectric production incentives for fy2014-fy2018.
