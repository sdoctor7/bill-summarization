the medical testing availability act of 2015 .
this bill amends the federal food, drug, and cosmetic act to require a product whose labeling bears the statement to be adulterated or misbranded under the federal food, drug, and cosmetic act to require the food and drug administration (fda) to not be deemed to be adulterated or misbranded it on the basis that the manufacturer or distributor of the product sells the product to an end user who uses the product in a manner inconsistent with such procedure.
