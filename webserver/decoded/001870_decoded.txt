pay down the debt act of 2015 .
this bill amends the congressional budget act of 1974 to require the office of management and budget (omb) to report to congress whether the projected debt held by the public to the estimated gross domestic product of the united states that is lower than the ratio in fiscal year 2016 is a ratio of debt held by the public to the estimated gross domestic product of the united states that does not exceed the ratio in the prior fiscal year.
