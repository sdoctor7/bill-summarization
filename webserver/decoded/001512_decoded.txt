the stop sanctuary cities act .
this bill prohibits any state or political subdivision of a state to receive funds from the state criminal alien assistance program under the immigration and nationality act. prohibits any state or political subdivision of a state that violates this act shall not be eligible to receive a grant under the byrne memorial justice assistance grant program established by the secretary of homeland security (dhs) to receive a grant under the byrne memorial justice assistance grant program (snap, formerly known as the food stamp program).
