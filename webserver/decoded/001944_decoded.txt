veterinary medicine mobility act of 2014 - amends the controlled substances act to allow a registrant who is a veterinarian to have a separate registration in order to transport and dispense controlled substances in the usual course of veterinary practice at a site other than the individual's principal place of business or professional practice so long as the site of transporting and dispensing is located in a state where the veterinarian is licensed to practice veterinary medicine and is not a principal place of business or professional others.
