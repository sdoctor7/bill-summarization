the article v records transparency act of 2016 .
this bill requires the archivist of the united states to make an organized compilation to the extent practicable, all applications and rescissions of congress, ever made by states under article v of the constitution to call a convention for proposing amendments to the constitution and transmit to congress that [UNK] together with both physical and electronic copies of each such application and [UNK] authorizes the archivist to use the records contained in the national archive and records administration and make all efforts feasible to obtain an official copy of any application
