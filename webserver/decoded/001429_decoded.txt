veterans' entry to apprenticeship act .
this bill directs the department of veterans affairs (va) to treat a pre-apprenticeship program as a program of apprenticeship for purposes of providing educational assistance, or in the case of a program for which a state does not require any such period, if the curriculum of the program is approved by a sponsor and the sponsor certifies to the secretary that the program will prepare an individual with skills and competencies needed to enroll in a registered apprenticeship program.
