the beach act of 2017 .
this bill amends the federal water pollution control act (commonly known as the clean water act) to revise requirements for the water pollution source identification of the department of health and human services (hhs) and the environmental protection agency (epa) to address identified sources of contamination by pathogens and pathogen indicators in coastal recreation waters adjacent to beaches or pathogen indicators in coastal recreation waters adjacent to beaches or similar points of access that are used by the internet.
