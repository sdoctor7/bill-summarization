expansion of u.s. combat troops into syria act .
this bill prohibits the deployment of members of the armed forces to be obligated or expended for the purpose of deploying members of the united states armed forces on to the ground of engaging in ground combat [UNK] awarding a contract to a private security contractor to conduct any activity on the ground of syria or otherwise establishing any presence of members of the u.s. armed forces or private security contractors on the ground of duty.
