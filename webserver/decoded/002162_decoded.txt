fuel reform act of 2013 - amends the clean air act to require the administrator of the energy information administration to: (1) determine the average monthly volume of cellulosic biofuel produced by such facility based on the actual volume produced by such facility during the period beginning on the date of initial production of cellulosic biofuel by the current calendar year, (2) determine the average monthly volume of cellulosic biofuel produced by such facility based on the actual volume produced by such facility and based on such average monthly volume produced by the date of initial production of cellulosic biofuel
