the engineering biology research and development act of 2015 .
expresses the sense of congress that the advisory committee designated under the engineering and planning act of 2002 regarding the office of national security, international states and development administration of the department of homeland security (dhs) shall carry out a coordinated federal research program to ensure continued united states leadership in engineering and to develop and implement a comprehensive research and development program to ensure continued united states leadership in engineering and to develop a comprehensive and advance existing strategy. requires the secretary to award grants to local educational agencies
