work requirements for welfare programs act of 2013 - prohibits the secretary of health and human services (hhs) from implementing, implementing, administer, or take any action to give effect to the information memorandum dated july 4, 2011, [UNK] in the information memorandum set forth in the information memorandum or that reflects the same or similar policies as those set forth in the information memorandum that waives compliance with a requirement of the enactment of this act.
