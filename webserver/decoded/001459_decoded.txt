this bill directs the department of the interior to conduct a special resource study of the archeological acreage of the new philadelphia archeological site and the surrounding land in illinois kansas. the bill authorizes the secretary of the interior to: (1) evaluate the national significance of the study area, (2) determine the suitability and feasibility of designating the study area as a unit of the national park system.
