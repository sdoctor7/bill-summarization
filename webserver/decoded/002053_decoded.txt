moapa band of paiutes land conveyance act .
this bill establishes for a land conveyance in the state of nevada for: (1) held in trust by the united states for the tribe of the bureau of land management and the bureau of land administered by the bureau of land management and the bureau of reclamation as generally depicted on the map as reservation expansion act; and (2) complete a survey of the boundary lines to establish the boundaries of the land taken into such land.
