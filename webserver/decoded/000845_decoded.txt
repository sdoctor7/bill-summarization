value based insurance design for better care act of 2015 or the vbid for better care act of 2015 .
this bill directs the speaker of the house of representatives and the president pro tempore of the senate to arrange for the presentation of a demonstration program requiring the utilization of insurance design to demonstrate that reducing the copayments or coinsurance charged to medicare beneficiaries for selected high-value prescription medications and clinical services can increase their utilization and ultimately improve clinical outcomes and lower health care providers.
