the terrorism prevention and critical infrastructure protection act of 2017 .
this bill directs the department of homeland security (dhs) to work with critical infrastructure owners and operators to take proactive steps to manage risk and strengthen the security and resilience of the nation's critical infrastructure against terrorist attacks that could have a debilitating impact on national security, economic management, public health and safety. the efforts must seek to reduce [UNK] minimize consequences and hasten response and recovery efforts related to critical communities.
