the taxpayer accountability act - directs the internal revenue service (irs) to provide a substantive written response to taxpayer information to any agency or instrumentality of federal, state, or local government, the internal revenue service (irs) shall provide a written notification to the taxpayer describing the information level, the taxpayer notification of disclosures by irs of taxpayer expense. any audit of a tax return of an individual by the internal revenue service (irs) shall not assess any tax with respect to which such audit relates after the conclusion of such procedure.
