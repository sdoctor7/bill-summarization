the national monument designation transparency act .
this bill amends the federal criminal code to require the president to not issue a proclamation before the last day of the 30-day period beginning on the date on which the president provides the language of the proposed proclamation to congress and to the governor of each state, the chief elected official of each unit of local government, and the governing entity of each tribal government with jurisdiction over parcels of the proposed national monument the chief elected official of each unit of local government, and the governing entity of each tribal government
