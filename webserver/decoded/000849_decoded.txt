autism services and workforce acceleration act of 2014 - amends title iv (student assistance) of the public health service act to require the department of health and human services (hhs) to establish a program to provide a comprehensive rights services for the national training initiatives on autism care programs demonstration plans. the bill amends the public health service act to require the department of health and human services (hhs) to award competitive subgrants to local educational agencies (leas) to support students' treatment for individuals with autism spectrum disorders and their families.
