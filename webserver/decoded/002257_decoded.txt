student privacy act of 2015 .
amends the family educational rights and privacy act of 1974 (erisa) to require any funds to be made available to any educational agency or institution that has not implemented information security policies and procedures that: (1) protect personally identifiable information from education records maintained by the educational agency or [UNK] and (2) require each outside party to whom personally identifiable information from education records is disclosed to have information security policies and procedures that include a comprehensive security program designed to protect the personally identifiable information from education hazards.
