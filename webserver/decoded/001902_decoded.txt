modernizing american manufacturing bonds act .
this bill amends the internal revenue code, with respect to qualified small issue bonds to include any facility used in the manufacturing or production of tangible personal property used in the creation or production of intangible property which is functionally related and subordinate to a facility described in which: (1) those facilities are located on the same site as the manufacturing facility and (2) not more than 50% of the net proceeds of the issue are used to provide those services.
