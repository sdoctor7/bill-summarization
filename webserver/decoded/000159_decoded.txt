the patient opportunity protection act of 2015 .
amends the public health service act to require the secretary of health and human services (hhs) to not take any action to require a hospital indemnity or other fixed indemnity insurance to be treated as an excepted benefit only if the benefits of the hospital indemnity or other fixed indemnity insurance are provided to an individual who has other health coverage that is minimum essential coverage within the preceding year. amends the internal revenue service act.
