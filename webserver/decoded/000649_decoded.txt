trauma systems and regionalization of emergency care reauthorization act .
this bill amends the public health service act to reauthorize certain trauma care programs for trauma care programs through fy2019. amends the public health service act to reauthorize through fy2022 the grant program for grants for trauma care response to trauma care and trauma care programs. the bill also amends the public health service act to reauthorize certain trauma care programs for trauma care programs through fy2020.
