mental health for foster youth act .
this bill amends part b (supplementary medical insurance) of title iv of the social security act to ensure that an initial mental health screening of any child in foster care be completed not later than 30 days after the child enters into foster care and in the case of any child in foster care for whom a mental health issue is identified in such initial screening that a comprehensive assessment of the mental health of the child be completed not later than 60 days after the child enters into foster care.
