the veterans legal support act of 2015 .
authorizes the secretary of veterans affairs (va) to provide support to one or more university law school programs that are designed to provide legal assistance to veterans. the va shall also provide support to the medical services account of the department of veterans affairs (va) programs that assist veterans with: (1) filing and appealing claims for benefits under laws administered by the secretary of veterans affairs, and (2) include financial support and family legal activities.
