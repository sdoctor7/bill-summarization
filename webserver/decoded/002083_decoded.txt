shareholder protection act of 2015 .
this bill amends the securities exchange act of 1934 to require shareholder authorization before a public company to make certain political expenditures and an individual who is an independent expenditure for purposes of this act. amends the federal election campaign act of 1971 to require the department of labor to carry out a program to provide political contributions and political expenditures and disclosure of votes of institutional investors in order to provide such services. requires the secretary of health and human services (hhs) to carry out a program to provide political contributions and expenditures
