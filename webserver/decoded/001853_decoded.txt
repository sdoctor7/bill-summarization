this bill amends the illegal immigration reform and immigrant responsibility act of 1996 to require an alien who is not lawfully present in the united states to not be eligible for any postsecondary education benefit unless every citizen and national of the united states is eligible to receive such a benefit if a citizen or national of the united states who is enrolled at a postsecondary educational institution in the united states that is alleged to have violated a citizen or national of the united states who is enrolled at a postsecondary educational institution in the united states.
