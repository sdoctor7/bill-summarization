coretta scott king mid-decade redistricting prohibition act of 2013 - authorizes the secretary of the treasury to provide for the fifteenth and subsequent decennial censuses and to provide for an apportionment of representatives in 2013, may not be redistricted again until after the next apportionment of representatives unless a court requires the state to conduct such subsequent redistricting to comply with the constitution or to enforce the voting rights act of 1965.
