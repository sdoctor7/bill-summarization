the natchez trace parkway land conveyance act of 2013 - directs the secretary of the interior to convey to the state of mississippi land that is located southeast of u.s. route 61/84 and which is commonly known as the bean field property to reserve an easement to reserve an easement to the united states restricting the use of the parcel to only those uses which are compatible with the natchez trace [UNK] the parcels of land referred to be adjusted to exclude the conveyed land.
