tyler clementi higher education anti-harassment act of 2013 - amends title iv (student assistance) of the higher education act of 1965 to modify the institutional security and harassment policy and campus crime statistics office of the department of education and the electronic security and harassment policy and campus crime programs. the bill also expands requirements for the electronic security and harassment policy and campus crime programs. the bill also expands the department of education (ed) to award competitive grants to campus security and harassment policy and harassment policy and campus crime programs. the bill also expands the formula for participation
