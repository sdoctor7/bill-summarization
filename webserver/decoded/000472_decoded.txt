leveling the playing field act - amends the tariff act of 1930 to facilitate the administration and enforcement of antidumping and countervailing duty orders [UNK] authorizes the administering authority or the commission in selecting the facts otherwise available or using an inference that is adverse to the interests of a party shall not be subject to judicial review if it is not required to possess, or make any adjustments to, a countervailable subsidy rate or weighted average dumping margin based on any assumptions about information the interested party would have provided if the interested party had complied with the request
