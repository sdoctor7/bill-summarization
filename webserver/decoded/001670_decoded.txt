federal disaster notification and payment protection act of 2016 .
this bill prohibits federal funds from being used to recoup a debt owed to the united states related to assistance provided to an individual or household under section 408 of the robert t. stafford disaster relief and emergency assistance act with respect to a major disaster declared by the president on or after january 1, 2020. amends the robert t. stafford disaster relief and emergency assistance act to require the president to establish a process to ensure that each individual and business applies for a category of federal disaster assistance
