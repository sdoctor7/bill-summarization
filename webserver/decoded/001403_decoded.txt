intelligence community oversight act of 2014 - amends the intelligence authorization act for fiscal year 2010 to require the head of each element of the intelligence community to provide the comptroller general with timely access to information concerning the programs and activities of the intelligence community for any federal, state, program enforcement, report or review for which the comptroller general to the fullest extent possible and provide timely responses to requests for information based on consultation with the comptroller general of the united states and to accommodate a request for access to specific information if the head of an element
