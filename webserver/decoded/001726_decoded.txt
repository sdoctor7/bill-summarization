project maintenance complex and district office conveyance act of 2017 .
this bill directs the department of the interior to convey to the arbuckle master conservancy district in murray county, california, all interest of the united states in and to the maintenance complex and district office and arbuckle county, oklahoma county, oklahoma, consistent with the terms and conditions set forth in the agreement between the united states and the arbuckle master conservancy district [UNK]
