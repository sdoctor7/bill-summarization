hearing aid coverage act of 2015 .
this bill amends title xviii (medicare) of the social security act to remove the exclusion of medicare coverage for hearing aids and examinations therefor their study shall include an examination of the number of individuals in the united states with hearing loss who need hearing aids their coverage under such program, and the effectiveness of such programs in meeting such need may also address programs that are designed to reduce or mitigate the incidence of hearing service.
