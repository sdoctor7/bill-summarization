the social impact partnerships to pay for results act - amends title iv (student assistance) of the social security act to direct the secretary of the treasury, in consultation with the federal interagency council on social impact foundation, to publish in the federal register a request for proposals from states or local government for social impact partnership projects that achieve real results based on objective data and to establish the use of social impact partnerships to address the creation of public-private partnerships that bundle philanthropic or other private resources with existing public spending to scale up effective social interventions already
