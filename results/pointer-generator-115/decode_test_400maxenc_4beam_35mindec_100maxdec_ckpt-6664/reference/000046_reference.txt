promote startups act of 2017 this bill amends the internal revenue code to increase the maximum tax deduction for business start-up expenditures from $ 5,000 to $ 15,000 and to increase the deduction for the organizational expenditures of corporations from $ 5,000 to $ 10,000 .
the bill also increases the ceilings for such expenditures over which the amount of the tax deduction is reduced .
