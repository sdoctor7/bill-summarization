status of the sixth amendment act of 2017 this bill requires a state that receives a department of justice ( doj ) office of justice programs grant to report annually certain information with respect to the following categories of offenses : ( 1 ) criminal offenses punishable by a prison term of more than one year ; ( 2 ) criminal offenses punishable by a prison term of one year or less , including misdemeanors , traffic , and municipal violations ; and ( 3 ) acts of juvenile delinquency punishable by a term of detention .
a state must report , for each offense category , the number of charges for which : ( 1 ) an individual was prosecuted , ( 2 ) a defendant waived the right to counsel , ( 3 ) a defendant was represented by publicly appointed counsel , and ( 4 ) a defendant was represented by counsel who was not publicly appointed .
doj must publish the reports on its website .
