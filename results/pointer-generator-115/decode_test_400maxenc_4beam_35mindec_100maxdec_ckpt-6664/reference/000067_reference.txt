strong families act this bill amends the internal revenue code to : ( 1 ) allow certain employers a business-related tax credit for a specified percentage ( not to exceed 25 % ) of the amount of wages paid to their employees during any period ( not exceeding 12 weeks ) in which such employees are on family and medical leave , ( 2 ) limit the allowable amount of such credit with respect to any employee to the product of the normal hourly wage rate of such employee for each hour ( or fraction thereof ) of actual services performed for the employer and the number of hours ( or fraction thereof ) for which family and medical leave is taken , and ( 3 ) terminate such credit five years after the enactment of this bill .
the government accountability office shall complete a study on the effectiveness of the tax credit for paid family and medical leave .
