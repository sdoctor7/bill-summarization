climate change adapt america fund act of 2017 this bill requires the department of the treasury to issue climate change obligations .
the proceeds from the debt obligations must be deposited in the adapt america fund established by this bill within the department of commerce .
commerce must carry out an investment program to fund projects for reducing economic , social , and environmental impact of the adverse effects of climate change .
the bill establishes the climate change advisory commission to : ( 1 ) establish recommendations , frameworks , and guidelines for the investment program ; and ( 2 ) identify categories of the most cost-effective investments and projects that emphasize multiple benefits to commerce , human health , and ecosystems .
