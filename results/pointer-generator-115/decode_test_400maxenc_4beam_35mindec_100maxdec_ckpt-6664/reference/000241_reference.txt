this bill terminates the designation of the islamic republic of pakistan as a major non-nato ally .
the president may not issue a separate designation of pakistan as a major non-nato ally until the president certifies that pakistan : is continuing to conduct military operations that are contributing to significantly disrupting the safe haven and freedom of movement of the haqqani network , has taken steps to demonstrate its commitment in preventing the haqqani network from using any pakistani territory as a safe haven , is actively coordinating with afghanistan to restrict the movement of militants along the afghanistan-pakistan border , and has shown progress in the arrest and prosecution of haqqani network senior leaders and mid-level operatives .
