this bill amends the servicemembers civil relief act to allow a service member to terminate a commercial mobile , telephone exchange , internet access , or multichannel video programming service contract at any time after the date the service member receives military orders to relocate for at least 90 days to a location that does not support such service contract .
( currently , such provision applies to a cellular telephone service or telephone exchange service contract . )
a service member shall return any provider-owned consumer premises equipment to the service provider not later than 10 days after the service is disconnected .
