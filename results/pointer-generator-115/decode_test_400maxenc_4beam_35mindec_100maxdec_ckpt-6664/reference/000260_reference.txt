southwestern oregon watershed and salmon protection act this bill withdraws specified federal land in curry and josephine counties in oregon from : ( 1 ) entry , appropriation , or disposal under the public land laws ; ( 2 ) location , entry , and patent under the mining laws , and ( 3 ) operation under the mineral leasing and geothermal leasing laws .
the bill amends the wild and scenic rivers act to revise the segment designations for the chetco river in oregon .
the federal land within the boundaries of such segments of the chetco river is withdrawn from : ( 1 ) entry , appropriation , or disposal under the public land laws ; ( 2 ) location , entry , and patent under the mining laws ; and ( 3 ) operation under the mineral leasing and geothermal leasing laws .
