this bill amends the intermodal surface transportation efficiency act of 1991 to designate the edward t. breathitt ( pennyrile ) parkway from its intersection with interstate route 24 to its intersection with interstate 69 and the western kentucky parkway as : ( 1 ) a high priority corridor on the national highway system , ( 2 ) a segment of the interstate system , and ( 3 ) interstate route i-169 .
