no budget , no pay act this bill withholds the salaries of members of a house of congress that has not agreed to a budget resolution for fy2018 by april 15 , 2017 , as required by the congressional budget act of 1974 .
salaries are withheld from april 16 , 2017 , until the house of congress agrees to a budget resolution or the last day of the 115th congress , whichever is earlier .
