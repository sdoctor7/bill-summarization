strengthen and fortify existing bridges act of 2017 or the safe bridges act of 2017 this bill directs the department of transportation to establish a program to assist states to rehabilitate or replace bridges found to be structurally deficient .
states shall use apportioned program funds for projects to rehabilitate and replace such bridges .
the federal share of project costs is 100 % .
prevailing rate of wage requirements for construction of federal-aid highways projects shall apply to such projects .
