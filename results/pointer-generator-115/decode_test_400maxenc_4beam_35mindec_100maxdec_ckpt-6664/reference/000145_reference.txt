grant 's law this bill amends the immigration and nationality act to require mandatory detention of any alien who is unlawfully present in the united states and arrested for inadmissible or deportable crimes .
the bill transfers release authority from the department of justice to the department of homeland security ( dhs ) .
dhs may release the alien to an appropriate authority for proceedings related to the arrest , but dhs must resume custody for any period that the alien is not in custody of the appropriate authority .
if the alien is not convicted of crimes for which the alien was arrested , dhs must continue to detain the alien until removal proceedings are completed .
dhs must complete the removal proceedings within 90 days .
