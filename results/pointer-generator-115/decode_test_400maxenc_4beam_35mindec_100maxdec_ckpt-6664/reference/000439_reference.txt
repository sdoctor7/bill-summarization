ancsa cook inlet region land conveyance finalization act of 2017 this bill requires the department of the interior to convey to cook inlet region , inc. ( ciri ) , an alaska native regional corporation , land ciri selects from specified areas to satisfy its claim to an additional 43,000 acres under the alaska native claims settlement act .
ciri is granted a right of notice and first refusal for federal land or property identified as excess in ciri 's region .
