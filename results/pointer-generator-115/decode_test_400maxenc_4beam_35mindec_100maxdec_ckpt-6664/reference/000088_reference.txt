oppose oversharing by presidents act or the oops act this bill amends the intelligence authorization act for fiscal year 2013 to require the national security council , in consultation with the department of justice and the office of the director of national intelligence , to ensure a mitigation process and protocols are in place if the president publicly discloses classified intelligence without notifying the congressional intelligence committees .
if the president authorizes the public disclosure of classified information , the president is responsible for notifying the congressional intelligence committees of that disclosure .
