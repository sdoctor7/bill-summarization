waterway lng parity act of 2017 this bill amends the internal revenue code to provide for an energy equivalent for a gallon of diesel , in the case of liquefied natural gas ( lng ) , for the purposes of the 29 cents per gallon inland waterways trust fund financing rate .
( the rate is used for the excise tax on fuel used in commercial transportation on inland waterways . )
the bill specifies that the energy equivalent of a gallon of diesel is 6.06 pounds of lng .
