( sec. 1 ) permits the grand lodge of the fraternal order of police and its auxiliary to sponsor as free public events on the capitol grounds : the 36th annual national peace officers ' memorial service , on may 15 , 2017 , to honor the law enforcement officers who died in the line of duty during 2016 ; and the national honor guard and pipe band exhibition , on may 14 , 2017 , to allow law enforcement representatives to exhibit their ability to demonstrate honor guard programs and provide for a bag pipe exhibition .
