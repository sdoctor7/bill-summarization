reducing existing costs associated with pharmaceuticals for seniors act of 2017 or the rxcap act of 2017 this bill amends title xviii ( medicare ) of the social security act to eliminate beneficiary cost-sharing above the medicare prescription benefit 's annual out-of-pocket threshold beginning in plan year 2018 .
under current law , the medicare prescription drug benefit provides coverage above that threshold only with cost-sharing in the form of either copayment or coinsurance .
