support our startups act of 2017 this bill amends the internal revenue code to increase the amount of the tax deduction for business start-up expenses for taxable years beginning in 2017 or 2018 .
under current law , start-up expenses are paid or incurred in connection with : ( 1 ) investigating the creation or acquisition of an active trade or business ; ( 2 ) creating an active trade or business ; or ( 3 ) any activity engaged in for profit and for the production of income before the day on which the active trade or business begins , in anticipation of the activity becoming an active trade or business .
