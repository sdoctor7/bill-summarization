military family leave act of 2017 this bill entitles an employed family member of a member of the armed forces who receives notification of a call or order to active duty in support of a contingency operation , or who is deployed in connection with a contingency operation , to two workweeks of leave per year for each family member who is so called or deployed .
such leave may : ( 1 ) be taken intermittently or on a reduced leave schedule ; and ( 2 ) consist of paid or unpaid leave , as the employer considers appropriate .
the bill : ( 1 ) allows an employer to require certification of entitlement to such leave within a leave request , ( 2 ) provides employment and benefits protection for employees upon their return from such leave , and ( 3 ) prohibits an employer from interfering with or otherwise denying the exercise of such leave rights .
