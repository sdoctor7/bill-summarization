fracturing regulations are effective in state hands act this bill gives states the sole authority to promulgate or enforce any regulation , guidance , or permit requirement regarding hydraulic fracturing on or under any land within their boundaries .
hydraulic fracturing or fracking is a process to extract underground resources such as oil or gas from a geologic formation by injecting water , a propping agent ( e.g. , sand ) , and chemical additives into a well under enough pressure to fracture the geological formation .
hydraulic fracturing on federal land must comply with the law of the state in which the land is located .
