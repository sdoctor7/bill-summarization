require evaluation before implementing executive wishlists act of 2017 or the review act of 2017 this bill prohibits a final agency rule from being published or taking effect until : ( 1 ) the agency submits the rule to the office of information and regulatory affairs ( oira ) , and ( 2 ) oira makes a determination as to whether the rule is a `` high-impact rule '' that may impose an annual cost on the economy of at least $ 1 billion .
an agency shall postpone the effective date of a high-impact rule until the final disposition of all actions seeking judicial review of the rule .
