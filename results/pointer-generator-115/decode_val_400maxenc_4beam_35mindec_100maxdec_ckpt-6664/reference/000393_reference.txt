honor guardsmen and reservists act this bill directs the department of veterans affairs ( va ) to make available for purchase a memorial headstone or marker for the marked or unmarked grave of : ( 1 ) an individual who performed inactive duty training or active duty for training for at least six years as a member of a national guard or reserve component , but did not serve on active duty and is not otherwise ineligible for a memorial headstone or marker on account of the nature of the individual 's separation from the armed forces or other cause ; or ( 2 ) such an individual whose remains are unavailable .
such a headstone or marker may be purchased under this subsection by : ( 1 ) the individual ; ( 2 ) the surviving spouse , child , sibling , or parent of the individual ; or ( 3 ) an individual other than the next of kin , as determined by the va. .
