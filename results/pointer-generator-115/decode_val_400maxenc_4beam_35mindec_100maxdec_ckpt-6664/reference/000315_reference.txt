outsourcing accountability act of 2017 this bill amends the securities exchange act of 1934 to require an issuer that is registered with the securities and exchange commission ( sec ) to disclose to the sec specified information related to the number of domestic and foreign employees of the issuer and its subsidiaries .
an issuer that is an `` emerging growth company '' ( in general , a newly public company with revenues below a specified threshold ) shall be exempt from the bill 's disclosure requirement .
