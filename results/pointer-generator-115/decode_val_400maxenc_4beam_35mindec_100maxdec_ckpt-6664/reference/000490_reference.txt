communicating lender activity reports from the small business administration act or the clear sba act this bill amends the small business act to define `` covered loan '' as a loan made or debenture issued under the act or the small business investment act of 1958 by a private individual or entity .
the bill requires the small business administration ( sba ) to make publicly available on its website a lender activity index ( i.e. , a user-friendly database of specified information relating to lenders making covered loans ) .
the sba shall : ( 1 ) include in the database information relating to covered loans made during fy2009-fy2016 , and ( 2 ) incorporate information related to such loans on an ongoing basis .
