fair access to science and technology research act of 2017 this bill requires each federal agency with extramural research expenditures of over $ 100 million to develop a federal research public access policy that is consistent with , and that advances , the purposes of the agency and that follows common procedures for the collection and depositing of research papers .
each federal research public access policy is applicable to : ( 1 ) researchers employed by the federal agency whose works remain in the public domain , and ( 2 ) researchers funded by the agency .
the bill specifies exclusions .
each federal agency must submit an annual report on its federal research public access policy to specified congressional committees .
