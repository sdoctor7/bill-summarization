this bill declares that the memorandum titled `` presidential memorandum regarding the hiring freeze '' published on january 23 , 2017 , or any other action by the president to prevent a department or agency from filling vacant civilian employee positions or creating new such positions , shall have no force or effect with respect to any department of defense ( dod ) civilian position under which an employee performs a dod function at a public shipyard or a dod function that directly supports a public shipyard .
