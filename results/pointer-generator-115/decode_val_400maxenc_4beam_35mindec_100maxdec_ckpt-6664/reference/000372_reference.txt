stop deceptive advertising for women 's healthcare services act this bill requires the federal trade commission ( ftc ) to promulgate rules to prohibit , as an unfair and deceptive act or practice , a person from advertising with the intent to deceptively create the impression that : ( 1 ) such person is a provider of abortion services if such person does not provide such services , or ( 2 ) such person is not a provider of abortion services if such person does provide such services .
`` abortion services '' is defined as providing surgical and non-surgical procedures to terminate a pregnancy , or providing referrals for such procedures .
the bill sets forth the enforcement authority of the ftc , including with respect to nonprofit organizations .
