honoring hometown heroes act ( sec. 2 ) this bill authorizes the governor of a state , territory , or possession of the united states or the mayor of the district of columbia to proclaim that the u.s. flag shall be flown at half-staff in the event of the death of a first responder ( public safety officer ) working in such jurisdiction who dies while serving in the line of duty .
