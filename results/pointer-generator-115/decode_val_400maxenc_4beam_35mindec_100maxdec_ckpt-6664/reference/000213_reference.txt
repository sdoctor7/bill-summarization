family act of 2017 this bill amends the internal revenue code to allow a tax credit for 50 % of an individual 's qualified infertility treatment expenses , subject to specified limits based on dollar amounts and the taxpayer 's adjusted gross income .
qualified infertility treatment expenses are amounts paid for the treatment of infertility via in vitro fertilization if such treatment is provided by a licensed physician , surgeon , or other medical practitioner and is administered with respect to a diagnosis of infertility by a physician licensed in the united states .
