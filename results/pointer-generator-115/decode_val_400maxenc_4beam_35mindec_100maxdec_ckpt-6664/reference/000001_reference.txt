science laureates of the united states act of 2017 this bill directs the national science foundation ( nsf ) to establish the position of science laureate of the united states to honor the service of scientists .
the nsf shall appoint a science laureate from three individuals to be nominated each year by the national academy of sciences on the basis of merit , particularly the ability to foster public awareness and interest in science and to provide ongoing significant scientific contributions .
each science laureate shall engage the public to increase public awareness about science and is encouraged to continue his or her scientific work .
