thurgood marshall 's elementary school study act this bill directs the department of the interior to conduct a special resource study of the public school located in west baltimore , maryland , that former supreme court justice thurgood marshall attended as a youth and any other resources in the surrounding neighborhood that relate to his early life .
interior shall : ( 1 ) evaluate the study area 's national significance ; and ( 2 ) determine the suitability and feasibility of designating it as a unit of the national park system .
