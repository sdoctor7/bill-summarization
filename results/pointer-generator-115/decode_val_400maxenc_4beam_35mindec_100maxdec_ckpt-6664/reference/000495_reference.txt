eastern band cherokee historic lands reacquisition act this bill takes specified lands and easements in monroe county , tennessee , into trust for the benefit of the eastern band of cherokee indians .
these lands include the sequoyah museum , the chota memorial , the tanasi memorial , and land to provide support for these properties and cultural programs .
the tva maintains its right to carry out river control and development on these lands , including temporarily and intermittently flooding certain lands .
the bill specifies the structures that may be constructed with the tva 's consent on certain lands subject to flooding .
the tva must be compensated for lost hydropower capacity from future development of these lands .
gaming on these lands is prohibited .
