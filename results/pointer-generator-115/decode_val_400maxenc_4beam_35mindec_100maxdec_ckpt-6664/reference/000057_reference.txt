original living wage act of 2017 this bill amends the fair labor standards act of 1938 to increase the federal minimum wage to the minimum hourly wage sufficient for a person working for 40 hours per week , 52 weeks per year , to earn an annual income 15 % higher than the federal poverty threshold for a four-person household , with two children under age 18 , and living in the 48 contiguous states .
the department of labor ( dol ) shall determine the minimum wage rate by june 1 , 2017 , and review it every four years .
dol may not adjust the minimum wage to a lower rate .
