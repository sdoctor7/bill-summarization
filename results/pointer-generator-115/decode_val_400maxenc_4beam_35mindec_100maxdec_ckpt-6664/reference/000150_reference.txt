responsible additions and increases to sustain employee health benefits act of 2017 this bill amends the internal revenue code , with respect to the tax exclusion for distributions from health flexible spending arrangements provided under a cafeteria plan , to : ( 1 ) increase the annual limit on employee salary reduction contributions to $ 5,000 , with an additional $ 500 for each additional employee dependent above two dependents that has not been taken into account by another person for the year ; ( 2 ) revise the adjustment for inflation after 2017 ; and ( 3 ) allow a carryforward into the next year for unused amounts in such plans .
