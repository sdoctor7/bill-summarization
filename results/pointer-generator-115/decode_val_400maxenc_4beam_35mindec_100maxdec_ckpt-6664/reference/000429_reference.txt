pension and budget integrity act of 2017 this bill prohibits provisions that increase or extend an increase of pension benefit guaranty corporation ( pbgc ) premiums from being counted as an offset to determine budget points of order for legislation in the house or the senate .
( the pbgc is a federal agency that insures the benefits of private sector , defined benefit pension plans .
the pbgc is financed by insurance premiums paid by sponsors of the plans , investment income , assets from pension plans taken over by the pbgc , and recoveries from the companies formerly responsible for the plans .
this bill prevents increases in pbgc premiums from being used to pay for provisions that increase the deficit in determining whether a budget point of order applies to legislation . )
