chemical poisons reduction act of 2017 this bill prohibits the use of sodium fluoroacetate ( i.e. , compound 1080 ) and sodium cyanide in a predator control device .
it imposes criminal penalties -- a fine , a prison term of up to two years , or both -- on a violator .
a predator control device is a dispenser , livestock collar , or gas cartridge that propels , releases , or emits sodium fluoroacetate or sodium cyanide for wildlife management or animal control purposes .
