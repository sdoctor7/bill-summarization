honoring our heroes act this bill permits , where there is no living immediate next of kin of a deceased member of the armed forces , a covered relative to request the one-time free replacement of certain military decorations of such deceased member .
`` covered relative '' means a relative of a deceased recipient of a military decoration who is : ( 1 ) not the immediate next of kin ; and ( 2 ) related within the fourth degree of consanguinity to the deceased recipient , or a more distant relative if determined appropriate .
