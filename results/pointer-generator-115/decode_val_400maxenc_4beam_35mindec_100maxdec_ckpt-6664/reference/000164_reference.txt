seat egress in air travel act of 2017 or the seat act of 2017 this bill directs the federal aviation administration to prescribe regulations : establishing minimum standards for space on passenger aircraft , including the size , width , and pitch of seats , the amount of leg room , and the width of aisles on such aircraft for the safety and health of passengers ; and requiring each air carrier to prominently display on its website the amount of space available for each passenger on aircraft it operates .
