tibetan refugee assistance act of 2017 this bill makes 3,000 immigrant visas available in fy2017-fy2019 for individuals who were born in tibet and have been continuously residing in india or nepal prior to the date of the enactment of this act .
an individual shall be considered to be a native of tibet if such individual was born in tibet or is the son , daughter , grandson , or granddaughter of an individual born in tibet .
the bill provides derivative status for accompanying/following spouses and children .
