restoring the partnership for county health care costs act of 2017 this bill amends titles xvi ( supplemental security income -lsb- ssi -rsb- ) , xviii ( medicare ) , xix ( medicaid ) , and xxi ( children 's health insurance program -lsb- chip -rsb- ) to allow an otherwise eligible individual who is in custody pending charges to receive ssi , medicare , medicaid , or chip benefits .
any ssi benefits payable to such an individual : ( 1 ) must be withheld until the individual is no longer in custody ; and ( 2 ) if the individual dies while in custody , shall be paid to the individual 's estate .
