buffalo bayou national heritage area act this bill establishes the buffalo bayou national heritage area in texas .
the buffalo bayou national heritage area corporation is designated as the management entity for the heritage area .
the corporation shall submit a management plan for the heritage area .
the bill sets forth requirements for the approval or disapproval of the plan .
the bill specifies its effect on private property protections and water rights with regard to the heritage area .
nothing in this bill is meant to modify the rio grande natural area act .
